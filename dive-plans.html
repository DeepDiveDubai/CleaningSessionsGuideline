Get Started

Sessions are planning based on spots, session time/repetation and divers' ability. That is why it is important to do the task which is assigned to you. If you want to change plan/roles that assigned you, you have to share this information with a reason. This is only way to track sessions.

Cleaning Dive Planning

Spot Base Planning

There are 3 main factor that effect spots.

  1. Dirt level
  2. Media schedule
  3. Collection points

Time/Repeatation Base Planning

Cleaning sessions can be performed early morning, working hours or after working hours. Dive plan will be plan according to times. That may be important to not change schedule after assigned cleaning session.

Ex. evening sessions we can brush and morning session we can vacoom that silt/dirt.

Divers Base Planning

There are 2 main variable about divers.

  1. Diver's certification (important for gas selection and decompression time)
  2. Daily schedule is also effects planing.
