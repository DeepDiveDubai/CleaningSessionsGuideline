Valves

In this section you will learn details and operational tips of valves in the pool.

There are 8 valves in the pool that connected to Deep Connected Machine. You can find their location below:

Unit Description Depth
2 in the right sidewalk top of the pipe 12m
2 in the right sidewalk bottom of the pipe 18m
2 aligned with mirror 30m
1 aligned with mirror 47m
1 aligned with mirror 54m

IMPORTANT! At least 1 valve must be open while the machine is running. If all valves are closed, the machine will be damaged.

Good Practise: Usually leave 30m valves open. The reason is these valves are using more than the others.
