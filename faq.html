F.A.Q.

Question #1

02/08/2023
Why we should not brush walls?

When we brush the walls, the particles formed stay suspended for a long time because they are not heavy. This situation causes the visibility to drop and the quality of the captured videos to decrease.

Also, when we look at the ratio of surface area and divers, you can see that there is no effective solution.

We are aware that the formation on the wall does not look good. We try different methods for this. Analyzes are being made and the search for a solution continues. If you have experience/opinion about this subject (other than brushing), you can share it with us.

Question #2

02/08/2023
Should I clean the place assigned to me? Can I clean another place?

When someone is assigned an area to clean, assignments are made based on the event time, priority and pollution status of the regions, the diver's daily calendar, and the diver's certification and experience. In this case, you must have a good reason to change. If you share this situation with the management, of course, you can clean a place other than the one assigned to you. We are not shepherds. We are self organize team.

If, for any reason, you think that you can't/don't want to do the dive assigned to you, please contact Nevcan.

Question #3

02/08/2023
Should I give feedback after every session?

I know this is an extra job for you. That's why I try to get a quick feedback verbally or via whatsapp instead of sending you long forms.

We are currently trying to create a new order. In order to see whether the things we do are working or not, it is necessary to collectively evaluate everything done from a broad perspective. Unless you give feedback, it is not possible for us to plan the efficiency of the work done or the work to be done. Therefore, it is very important to give feedback until a general order is established.

Question #4

02/08/2023
Why algaes should clean?

It can spread when underwater fauna is not kept under control. In order to prevent this situation and keep it under control, algae and similar fungus formations should be cleaned regularly.
